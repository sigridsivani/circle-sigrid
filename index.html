A tweet is a short digital message posted on the social media website and app X, formerly known as Twitter, and is used by people globally to communicate short-form ideas within a character limit (currently 280). The contents of a tweet is not confined to words, but also allows users to share videos, images, links, as well as a reply to or repost (retweet) of an original post (OP). Each tweet is a standalone data entity with metadata displayed around the message itself such as timestamp, location, authors username and image, likes, replies and retweets
