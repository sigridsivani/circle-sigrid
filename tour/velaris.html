Velaris

“ I love my people, and my family. Do not think I wouldn’t become a monster to keep them protected. ”
–Rhysand, A court of Thorns and Roses


Velaris, also known as the Court of Dreams and the City of Starlight, is a hidden city on the western side of the Night Court of Prythian.

Its inhabitants agree that Rhysand be their High Lord despite the fact that he is not fully High Fae being half Illyrian, whom their counterparts the inhabitants of the Court of Nightmares consider Lesser Faeries, it is for this reason that the inhabitants of said Court do not approve of him.

Velaris is ruled and potected by Rhysand and his closest friends, the inner circle. The inner circle consists oh himself, High Lady Feyre, Amren, an otherwordly creature trapped in a fae body, who is his second-in-command, Morrigan,who is the Third-in-Command and the overseer of the Court of Nightmares, she is also Rhysand's cousin, Azriel, shadowsinger and spymaster, and Cassian, the General Commander of the Night Court's armies. Eventually Feyre's sisters Nesta and Elaine are also a part of the inner circle.
